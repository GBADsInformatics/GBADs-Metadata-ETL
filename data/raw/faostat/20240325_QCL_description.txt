The "Production: Crop and livestock statistics" dataset provides statistics for 278 products collected by the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO). Data was obtained from the FAOSTAT (Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations Corporate Statistical Database) database (domain code QCL) and filtered to include only livestock population numbers. Data is reported as the number of heads (number of animals). The full metadata from the data source is available here: https://www.fao.org/faostat/en/#data/QCL/metadata
